Tesla Notes

Use the Tesla trip planner — it auto-routes Supercharger stops and pre-conditions the battery for faster charging. Always aim to arrive with 10–15% remaining.
Expect roughly 220 miles of real range on the highway with AC, full load, and passengers. The app accounts for this — trust its estimates.
A V3 Supercharger adds ~150 miles in 20 minutes. Time each stop with bathroom and snack breaks — the kids will need them every two hours regardless.

Traveling with Kids

Prepare a small bag for each child with a few new, unseen activities — sticker books, a small toy, a drawing pad. Reveal one every couple of hours.
Schedule your longest stretches during the 12:30–3pm window. The two-year-olds especially will sleep right through in the car.
Book suites over standard rooms. Residence Inn, Embassy Suites, and Home2 Suites all have separate living areas — eight people in one room is untenable.

Notes

Choose one activity in Albuquerque — Explora or the Zoo. There isn't time for both and still reach Amarillo before dark. For ages 2–6, Explora is the better fit.
Pick up a can of spray paint before the Cadillac Ranch exit — there's a shop on the I-40 frontage road right near the turnoff.
The Gallup charger has consistently poor reviews for speed. Don't rely on it for a full charge — just enough to comfortably reach Albuquerque.

Optional Morning

🏜️
Palo Duro Canyon
Open at 7:30am, 30 minutes south of Amarillo. Even a short drive through the canyon is worth the stop — the scale and color of the rock is extraordinary. Back on I-40 by 10am, still arriving in Shawnee early afternoon.
4.8 · 10,000+ reviews · 7:30am–10pm · $8 adult · Canyon, TX
Navigate

Notes

The easiest day of the trip — only 290 miles. There's no reason to rush. Sleep in, enjoy the hotel breakfast, take your time. You'll still arrive in Shawnee early afternoon.
If you visit Palo Duro Canyon, go before 9am. The canyon is cooler in the morning and the light is beautiful. Even the scenic drive without hiking takes only 30 minutes.
